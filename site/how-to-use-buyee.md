# How to Use Buyee to Buy from Japan

## Problem
Many overseas users find Japanese products online but cannot purchase them because Japanese shops do not ship internationally.

## What is Buyee
Buyee is a proxy shopping service that allows you to buy products from Japanese websites and have them shipped to your country.

## Step-by-step guide

1. Go to a Japanese online store and find the product you want.
2. Copy the product page URL.
3. Open Buyee and paste the URL into the search box.
4. Add the item to your cart and complete the payment.
5. Buyee receives the item in Japan and ships it to you.

## Why this is useful
This method allows you to access Japanese products that are normally unavailable outside Japan.

## Tools you need
- Buyee
- Amazon Japan
- Google Translate

## FAQ

**Do I need to understand Japanese?**  
No. You only need to copy and paste the URL.

**Is Buyee safe?**  
Yes. It is widely used by international customers.
